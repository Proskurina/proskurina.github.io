Red Green Refactor

03 September 2015

Today we've been given a task of researching what "Red Green Refactor" is. Basically it is the motto of TDD.

There are 3 simple steps:

Red - making your test fail

Green - writing a piece of code to make your test pass

Refactor - cleaning up you mess aka DRY (making your code look nicer AND work)

Simple enough process but quite powerful!

Thanks Archie for making it nice and simple! You are the best!
