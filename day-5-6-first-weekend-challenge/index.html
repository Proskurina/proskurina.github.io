Day 5-6 First weekend challenge

06 September 2015

I can not believe it is already Sunday and it is 11 pm!!!

I just sat on a sofa with my computer 5 minutes ago and it was only Saturday, 9 am.

That's all I want to say about our first weekend challenge.

Kidding. It was not so bad. It covered everything we had done during the week. And it was good to go through all materials one more time. I hate tests and I love tests. They are awful. But they help a lot. If you have 20 tests, and then do a little change in your program and suddenly all 20 do not pass, it is a nice little hint that something might be wrong with what you are doing.

But all end up well. And Travis is my new best friend.

Now to sleep.

Z-z-z-Z-Z-z-z...

Yana Proskurina
