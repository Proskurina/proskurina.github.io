Days 56-57

27 October 2015

This week we have Makerthon!

It is a warm-up before our final projects.

In the beginning of the week we had a brainstorming session on sharing various ideas of what could be done during the week. Some of the ideas were:

  • to make a todolist that can be used by several people, with shared goals,
  • to write a twitter with messenger,
  • do something Halloween themed,
  • to make a plug-in for Chrome that will let you close opened windows/tabs by whistling,
  • to make an on-line game,
  • make it possible to open the entrance door from Slack, and many-many others.

I loved the idea about whistling, but I had no clue how to approach it and if it is even doable!

There were two more guys who loved the idea and we formed an absolute dream team!

Guess what? We did it!

Try it here. Add it to Chrome, open a new window (not a tab, sorry still have a couple of bugs to catch), allow mic, open more tabs, browse in Internet and if someone enters the room... whistle! All tabs will be closed in a moment! Of course you were not procrastinating in Facebook!

Yana Proskurina
