Day 43 - Ada Lovelace Day

13 October 2015

Today is Ada Lovelace day.

Every girl in tech knows that she was the first computer programmer in the world! It might be true. At least it is nice to believe it. Here is a good article about her.

There are other great women in tech like Margaret Hamilton and Sandi Metz.

And there are also men.

And I do not see any reasons to believe that gender somehow influences the abilities to code. It is equal pain and fun for both.
