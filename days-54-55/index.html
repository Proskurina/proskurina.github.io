Days 54-55

25 October 2015

Last Friday formal part of the course have finished. We still have 4 weeks ahead, but no fixed agenda, we will choose what to learn by ourselves depending on our final projects.

So I decided to make the list of books that helped me a lot during these 8 weeks. I am sure I will come back to these books many times after the course.

  • Well-grounded Rubyist by David A. Black (pre-course)
  • Practical object oriented design in Ruby aka POODR by great Sandi Metz (1st and 2nd weeks)
  • Jump start Sinatra by Darren Jones (3rd and 4th weeks)
  • HTML/CSS and JavaScript/jQuery by Jon Duckett (5th and 6th weeks) - good books, but too many pictures, and as a result no kindle version and too heavy to carry
  • Ruby on rails tutorial by Michael Hartl and The rails 4 way by Obie Fernandez, Kevin Faustino (7th week)
  • AngularJS Up & Running by Shyam Seshadri, Brad Green (8th week)

Have a good read!

Yana Proskurina
