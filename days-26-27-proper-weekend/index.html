Days 26-27. Proper weekend!

27 September 2015

This week we built a clone of Twitter!

Well, very simple version of it, but still...

I think it was the most difficult weekend challenge so far. I started on Saturday at 9 am.

At 2 pm I had to have a break as I had tickets to Dismaland and needed to catch 3pm train from Paddington.

I continued in a train. And a couple of times I was so stuck that I was literally ready to cry. When you have an error and you have no clue how to deal with it! And so many files and folders so you cannot figure out which one could be failing... And no internet connection... And you do not know where you are and at what station you need to change the train!

But I finished it on my way back! And I have the whole Sunday for myself! For the first time since the course has started!

It was not so bad after all!

Yana Proskurina
