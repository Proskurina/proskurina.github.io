Day 8 - Fantastic day!

08 September 2015

Today started with a research that you can see in my previous post, and somehow it took sooooo much time, that we were not able to start Battle Ships before lunch.

During lunchtime yesterday and today we had Lunch talks. One more great thing in the Makers!

Yesterday as a guest we had a Director of CREATE Education Project. He told us a lot of pretty amazing stuff about 3D printing and how Ultimaker 3D Printers can be used for education.

And today the founder of Lexoo, who also have a huge experience in development, gave us couple of advises on how to become a good developer (write no code!).

After the talk and meditation we came back to our main project.

And I am so proud of myself today, because:

  • I managed to go slow, and go through each detail in MVP,
  • I managed to explain to my partner some very important things about testing,
  • We had a dramatic breakthrough in Battle Ships!!!

What a happy day!

On the way home was thinking about my progress...

And then saw this!

Is it just me, or is there a clear connection here?
I wonder what my result will be!
