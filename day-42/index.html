Day 42

12 October 2015

In the morning we met our juniors for the first time!
Only 6 weeks ago we were on their place. 6 weeks! Like the whole life! And a good one!

Now we have new coaches, they are very cool, but I will miss our old ones, they were geniuses.

This week we are going learn Ruby on rails. It is not that we really can learn it in a week, but as always we will give it a try and do our best.

Now the only thing I can say about it is MAGIC! It knows everything. Do not try to do anything your way, Ruby on rails knows better! I think it is the centrepiece of our course. So, the week should be very interesting (as if we've ever had a not interesting one).

And in the evening I went to Dee Dee Bridgewater concert! She was fantastic! I must admit that the course intensity is tough but bearable, it is my social life intensity that is killing me.

Yana Proskurina
